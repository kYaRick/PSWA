About ME

Well, growing up in Mlyniv I had always been active in the outdoors. After school I had worked some time for a large Vodafone company. In 2016, I graduated from art school and became interested in the Arduino platform So in 2017, I saw an opportunity to merge three of my passions, software, drawing and cycling. I had noticed a trend emerging in single speed bikes with bright colors that people had built up themselves. I thought this was a natural fit for customization. With a small initial investment, I dove in, leveraging my skills in software and ecommerce.
